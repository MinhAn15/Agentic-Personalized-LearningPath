Key Innovations

1. Tree of Thoughts Planning (Agent 3)

Instead of guessing, the Planner simulates future paths.


def plan_path(learner_state, course_graph):
    candidates = get_unlockable_concepts()
    
    # Simulate possible futures
    for concept in candidates:
        prob_success = simulate_mastery(concept, learner_state)
        score = pedagogical_value(concept) * prob_success
        
    return max(candidates, key=lambda x: x.score)

2. Socratic Pedagogical Enforcer (Agent 4)

Strict adherence to Harvard's 7 Principles.

"Do not give the answer. Guide the learner to discover it by connecting to what they already know (Learner KG)."
